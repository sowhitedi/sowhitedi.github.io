New Role: Vice President AIGA Miami

March 23, 2015

This happened awhile back (October 2014 to be precise) but I’m happy to make it official here on the blog: I’m proud to serve with the AIGA Miami board of directors as the new Vice President. The board is made up of a diverse group of design entrepreneurs, leaders, &t educators, and we all serve as volunteers, in addition to our full time gigs.

Since joining the board, I’ve been working to support AIGA Miami’s ongoing efforts to build up the design community in our city. I’m especially interested in projects that empower designers to do their best work and get the respect and pay they deserve.

I’m proud to be organizing a talk for next Monday evening (March 30), PARALLELS Legal Matters, which is an exploration of the legal issues that are critical for designers, entrepreneurs, and startups. This is an area of design practice & entrepreneurship that I’m very passionate about…and I think is too often neglected.

Here are some factors that help me answer the question, “Should I Take this Job?” These factors help me choose work that is a good fit for my needs and my working style.

PARALLELS Legal Matters will bring together two speakers, local attorney Christopher DiSchino, and U.N. Consultant Haydee Izaguirree. Topics will include: trademarks & the designer's responsibility; proper business setup for entrepreneurs; and how to deal with policy, bureaucracy, and legal issues – while still doing outstanding design work.

You can learn more about AIGA Miami and the PARALLELS talk (including tickets) here.
