child abuse prevention psa

statewide campaign inlcluding posters, billboards, radio, tv

I wrote, designed, creative-directed, and project-managed these PSA campaigns for the Mississippi Department of Human Services. One campaign focused on creating a safe sleep environment for infants; the other discussed the dangers of leaving a child alone in a hot car.

Television, radio, and billboards went statewide during the month of April. Posters were distributed to doctor's offices, health departments, and WIC offices all across Mississippi.

Working with Aaron Phillips of Aaron Phillips Photography, and Robby Piantanida of Borrowed Productions, we produced these campaigns using all local actors, voice talent, music, sound editing, and print vendors.

Design by So White; video by Borrowed Productions and photography by Aaron Phillips Photography.
