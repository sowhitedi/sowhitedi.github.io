REBECCA WHITE is an INDEPENDENT DESIGNER IN MIAMI.

So White Design & Illustration is my design studio based in Miami, Florida

I create functional design solutions for a broad range of clients, including startups, non-profits, state organizations, higher education, and artists and musicians. I do a lot of direct client work, but I also frequently collaborate with other designers and creative teams.

I specialize in an elegant and balanced aesthetic, Spanish-language design, off-beat illustrations, accurate estimates, and excellent client relationships. I’m equally comfortable working in print and web (including HTML/CSS as well).
