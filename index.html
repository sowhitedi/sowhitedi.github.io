SO WHITE DESIGN & ILLUSTRAION is an INDEPENDENT DESIGN STUDIO BASED IN MIAMI, FLORIDA.

Rebecca White is the founder and lead designer of So White Design & Illustration. She also is honored to serve as Vice President of the board of AIGA Miami.

We create functional design solutions for a broad range of clients, including startups, non-profits, state organizations, higher education, and artists and musicians. We work directly with clients, and we also collaborate with larger creative and development teams.

We specialize in an elegant and balanced aesthetic, Spanish-language design, off-beat illustrations, accurate estimates, and excellent client relationships.

So White Design & Illustration offers: UX / UI design, interface design, custom illustration, custom icons, copywriting and content, social media, project management, and broadcast. Check out some of our past projects here.
